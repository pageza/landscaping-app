About LandscapePro

For over 15 years, we've been transforming outdoor spaces and helping homeowners create beautiful, sustainable landscapes that enhance property value and quality of life.

Our Story

Founded in 2008 by landscape architect Sarah Mitchell, LandscapePro began with a simple mission: to make professional landscaping services accessible, reliable, and environmentally conscious.

What started as a one-person operation has grown into a full-service landscaping company serving over 2,000 satisfied customers across the region. We pride ourselves on combining traditional craftsmanship with modern sustainable practices.

Today, our team of certified landscapers, arborists, and designers work together to create outdoor spaces that are not just beautiful, but also functional and environmentally responsible.
